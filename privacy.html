4. Information We Collect & How We Use It

We only collect data that is strictly necessary to provide, optimize, and secure our local e-commerce services. This collection falls into the following categories:

A. Account and Authentication Data

To access ordering capabilities, users must authenticate. User authentication is securely managed using Firebase Authentication. Passwords are handled by Firebase according to Google's security infrastructure. Alternatively, if you log in via Google Sign-In, we receive tokenized profile information (name, email, and profile picture URL) authorized by your Google account configuration.

B. Transaction and Payment Information

Presently, ARS BAZZAR operates exclusively on a Cash on Delivery (COD) fulfillment model. We do not collect, process, or store credit card numbers, bank account details, or net banking credentials. To support future technical scalability, updates may introduce Unified Payments Interface (UPI) and other digital payment aggregators. Any such integration will occur through secure, PCI-DSS compliant third-party SDKs, and this policy will be updated accordingly.

C. User-Generated Content (UGC)

Our application does not include public-facing profiles, social walls, or community commentary zones. User-generated content is strictly restricted to functional feedback mechanisms and private Customer Support Chat messages. This transactional text is stored securely in our database to help resolve your operational inquiries.

D. Product Media Hosting

We integrate Cloudinary infrastructure for optimized delivery of product imagery. Currently, Cloudinary is used only for hosting administrator-uploaded product images. No personal user media is currently uploaded.

5. Android OS Permissions & Runtime Disclosures

In accordance with the Google Play User Data Policy, we provide transparent declarations regarding the explicit permissions our App requests. We never scan unrelated storage directories, silently monitor background actions, or sell permission-gated data assets.

android.permission.INTERNET

Purpose: Fundamental core permission. Allows the application to communicate with our Firebase back-end services, retrieve online product listings, process e-commerce orders, and synchronize transactional states.

android.permission.ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE

Purpose: Enables the app to check whether an active internet connection exists. This prevents data fragmentation, optimizes network requests, and gracefully alerts you if your device goes offline.

android.permission.READ_EXTERNAL_STORAGE
android.permission.WRITE_EXTERNAL_STORAGE

Purpose: On compatible legacy versions of Android, these permissions facilitate file workflows. On modern iterations of Android, ARS BAZZAR utilizes native app-specific storage and scoped access methods that do not compromise broader device storage data. Device storage access is confined strictly to exporting your billing invoice PDFs to designated system directories and writing internal application diagnostic logs. ARS BAZZAR never scans unrelated files, photos, or media on your device, and never uploads files without explicit user action.

android.permission.READ_CONTACTS
android.permission.WRITE_CONTACTS

Purpose: Requested explicitly to facilitate verified customer care channels. These permissions allow the app to save the official ARS BAZZAR Support Number directly into your device contact list, and verify if that official record already exists. This design protects you from malicious third parties attempting phishing or impersonating our brand. The App does not harvest, upload, analyze, distribute, synchronize, or sell your personal address book data.

android.permission.ACCESS_COARSE_LOCATION
android.permission.ACCESS_FINE_LOCATION

Purpose: Location permission is requested ONLY when the user voluntarily chooses to add or update a delivery address. Accurate coordinate data ensures that our local delivery agents can locate your drop-off point seamlessly. No continuous tracking occurs, location is never monitored in the background, and data is never sold to advertising networks.

android.permission.POST_NOTIFICATIONS

Purpose: Used to deliver critical real-time alerts regarding order updates, support replies, and optional promotional notifications or localized market offers. Users retain total control and can toggle this permission off at any time via Android System Settings.

android.permission.FOREGROUND_SERVICE
android.permission.FOREGROUND_SERVICE_DATA_SYNC

Purpose: Governs an optional user-activated feature called Live Support Sync. When enabled, it executes a visible foreground service to keep your active customer service chat instance synchronized in real-time and deliver instant message alerts while the app interface is minimized. Notice: Enabling this feature may increase device battery consumption. This service is never utilized for background surveillance, advertising, or unauthorized background tracking.

6. Cookies and Similar Technologies

However, underlying integrations including Firebase SDKs and Google Play Services may automatically deploy localized hardware device identifiers, analytical tokens, or temporary instance IDs. These are strictly used to authenticate user roles, secure data transmission channels, monitor performance thresholds, and keep system functionality working reliably.

9. Foreground Service Architecture & Battery Impact

Our app respects standard mobile computing restraints. The integration of FOREGROUND_SERVICE_DATA_SYNC is entirely dependent on user preference for interactive support. It presents a persistent, non-dismissible system notification while active, ensuring you are fully aware that a secure sync pipeline is running. You may close the support channel at any time to instantly terminate this foreground process and eliminate any supplementary battery draw.

11. Information Security Standards

We implement multi-layered security safeguards to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction:

  • Transport Layer Security: All data transmissions between the ARS BAZZAR client application and our cloud back-ends are strictly enforced over encrypted HTTPS/TLS pathways.
  • Identity Protection: Firebase Authentication protocols protect administrative access tiers, ensuring your operational profiles remain isolated.
  • Infrastructure Control: Database read/write parameters are strictly regulated via server-side security logic. Only vetted corporate administrators can access backend panels to fulfill orders.

Disclaimer: We use industry-standard security measures and continuously improve our security practices. We do not make claims regarding unverified cryptographic methods.
